# cfssl Overview

## What it is

1. Alternative to OpenSSL and easyRSA
1. created and maintained by Cloudflare
1. PKI/TLS toolkit used by Cloudflare
1. requires Go1.12+ to build

## Terms

1. certificate transparency
1. CRL (certificate revocation list)
